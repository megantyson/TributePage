Birmingham

Birmingham is a major city in the UKâ€™s West Midlands region, with multiple Industrial Revolution-era landmarks that speak to its 18th-century history as a manufacturing powerhouse. By the Victorian era, the city was known as the 'Workshop of the World'. Birmingham is also home to a network of canals, many of which are now lined with trendy cafes and bars. In fact, there are more miles of canals in Birmingham than in Venice!

Things to do in Birmingham:

  • Check out the Birmingham Museum and Art Gallery, known for its pre-Raphaelite masterpieces.
  • Head to the Birmingham Botanical Gardens to experience stunning glasshouses and feel as though you've stepped into a tropical rainforest.
  • Find the best view of the skyline at the Library of Birmingham's rooftop garden.

You can find more information about the city via Visit Birmingham.
